Salary

We are pleased to inform you that your salary will be ₹ {{ annual_ctc }} per annum. Please note that this salary is subject to adjustment in accordance with our employee compensation policies, as detailed in Annexure A.

Introductory Period

There will be an initial 180-day introductory period. During this period, employees are considered essential employees. After completing the introductory period, the individual will become a regular full-time employee. Employees are eligible to use Paid Time Off (PTO) from their 180th day of employment.

Tax Advice

You are encouraged to obtain your own tax advice regarding your compensation. You agree that the Company does not have a duty to design its compensation policies in a manner that minimises your tax liabilities, and you will not make any claim against the Company or its Board of Directors related to tax liabilities arising from your compensation.

Conflict of Interest & Background Verification

As part of your commitment to the Company, we request that during your employment you refrain from engaging in any other employment, consulting, or business activity (whether full-time or part-time) that may create a conflict of interest with GDS.

As part of our standard procedures, we conduct a thorough verification process covering your character, antecedents, previous employment testimonials, educational qualifications, and references. At any point, if information provided by you is found to be incorrect, suppressed, or false, or if you are convicted of criminal activities or found guilty of financial irregularities, your employment may be terminated immediately.

Separation

As per GDS separation policy, your resignation will be considered valid only when officially communicated to both your reporting manager and the HR Team. The employee has the right to terminate the employment agreement by providing a written notice of at least {{ notice_period }}. We understand that individual circumstances may vary and are open to negotiating a shorter notice period where reasonable.

Notice Period

GDS may or may not provide a {{ notice_period }} notice period in the event that termination of employment is intended. This notice period is applicable in cases including but not limited to:

  1. Reference verification found unacceptable.
  2. Employee found engaged in misrepresentation, misconduct, dishonesty, or moral turpitude.
  3. Employee's failure to perform duties as reported by the client reporting manager or based on the client's dissatisfaction or request for removal.
  4. Employee convicted of, or having pleaded guilty to, a felony or crime involving embezzlement, conversion of property, or fraud.
  5. Employee's failure to abide by GDS or client work rules, code of conduct, or policies.
  6. Non-availability to report to work for more than three days without informing the reporting manager or HR.

Benefits

Group Medical Coverage (GMC): ₹ 3,00,000 per annum (self).
Personal Accident Insurance: ₹ 10,00,000 per annum (self).

Leave

Employees are entitled to one privilege leave per calendar month (12 days per year), which may be carried forward if unutilised.

Right to Hire

Depending upon your performance, the client or engagement team has the right to hire you directly as a full-time employee, subject to mutual agreement.

Project Start Date
